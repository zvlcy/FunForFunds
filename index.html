What is FunForFunds


FunForFunds is a charity event with goal to raise awareness of the youth while bringing in donations for people in need. We bring talented singers from UK (Ed Sheeran), Indonesia (Nissa Sabyan) and our local singer (Faizal Tahir) and band (K-Clique). We are looking to be a part of charity community in Malaysia. We desire to raise awareness and solidarity in youth throughout the country for the betterment while having fun and interacting with well-known celebrities.
